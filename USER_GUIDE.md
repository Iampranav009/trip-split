## ðŸŽ¯ Quick Start Guide

### For New Users

1. **Login**
   - Click "Continue with Google" for quick setup
   - Or use "Sign Up" with email and password
   
2. **Customize Your Profile**
   - Navigate to Profile tab (bottom right)
   - Click "Settings"
   - Choose an avatar or upload your own
   - Update your name and phone number
   - Click "Save Changes"

3. **Create Your First Trip**
   - Click the "+" button on home screen
   - Enter a trip name (e.g., "Goa Trip 2024")
   - You're automatically the trip admin!

### Trip Management

#### Creating & Managing Expenses

1. **Add an Expense**
   - Open your trip
   - Click the big "+" button at the bottom
   - Enter:
     - Title (e.g., "Hotel Booking")
     - Amount
     - Date
     - Who paid
   - Choose split type:
     - **Equal**: Everyone pays the same
     - **Exact**: Set specific amounts for each person
     - **Percentage**: Assign percentages (must total 100%)
   - Save!

2. **Edit/Delete Expense**
   - Tap any expense in the list
   - Edit details and save
   - Or click the trash icon to delete

#### Inviting Friends to Your Trip

**Method 1: Share Link**
1. Open your trip
2. Click "Share" button (blue, top row)
3. Click "Copy" to copy the link
4. Share via your preferred method

**Method 2: QR Code**
1. Open your trip
2. Click "Share" button
3. Show the QR code to your friend
4. They scan with their phone camera

**Method 3: Direct Sharing**
1. Open your trip
2. Click "Share" button
3. Choose:
   - WhatsApp (opens WhatsApp with pre-filled message)
   - Email (opens email client)

#### Approving Join Requests (Admin Only)

When someone requests to join your trip:

1. You'll see a red notification badge on "Requests" button
2. Click "Requests" button
3. Review each request showing:
   - User's name
   - Email
   - Profile picture
   - Request time
4. Click:
   - "Approve" (green) - Add them to the trip
   - "Reject" (red) - Decline the request

Once approved, the user:
- Automatically becomes a trip member
- Can view all expenses
- Can see real-time updates
- Can add expenses (if you allow)

### Generating Invoices

**When to use:**
- End of trip to see final settlements
- Sharing expense summary with everyone
- Record keeping

**How to generate:**
1. Open your trip
2. Click "Invoice" button (green, top row)
3. PDF downloads automatically
4. Invoice includes:
   - Trip name and dates
   - Total expenses
   - Member-wise breakdown (who paid what)
   - Balance summary (who owes whom)
   - Settlement instructions (minimized transactions)
   - Full expense list with dates
   - Invite code for reference

**Pro Tip:** Share this invoice via email for transparency!

### Viewing Balances & Settlements

1. Open your trip
2. Click "Balance" tab at bottom
3. See:
   - **Net Balances**: Who's owed money vs who owes
   - **Settlement Instructions**: Minimized payment plan
   
4. **Mark Paid** button:
   - Click when a settlement is complete
   - Creates a settlement expense
   - Updates balances automatically

### Managing Members

1. Click "Members" card in trip dashboard
2. View all members with:
   - Name and avatar
   - Current balance (owes/receives)
   - Settlement status
3. Add new members manually:
   - Enter name
   - Choose avatar
   - Click "Add"

**Note:** Members added via invite request are added automatically!

### Profile & Settings

**Profile Tab:**
- View your details
- See profile picture

- Logout

**Settings:**
- **Profile Picture**: 
  - Choose from default avatars
  - Upload custom photo (auto-compressed)
- **Display Name**: Update how you appear to others

## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting

**Can't see join request button?**
- Only trip admins (creators) see this button

**Invite code not working?**
- Make sure to copy the entire code
- Try sharing the direct link instead

**Balance not updating?**
- Refresh the page
- Check internet connection
- Firebase syncs in real-time when connected

**Can't upload profile picture?**
- Check file size (should auto-compress)
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
- Try a different image

**PDF not downloading?**
- Check browser's download settings
- Allow downloads from the site
- Try a different browser
